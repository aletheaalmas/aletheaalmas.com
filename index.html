My Story

Hi there! I’m an Indonesian living in Zhengzhou, China.

Back in university, I chose Mandarin as my major without realizing how much it would shape my life. It opened so many doors, both in my career and personally. Learning Chinese even led me to meet my husband and discover countless new experiences.

In 2019, I started working as a Personal Assistant, a role that made me a true generalist since I worked with all kinds of departments to keep things running smoothly.

Later, I joined a SaaS startup where I wore multiple hats, from translating between teams, researching users and competitors, testing new features, preparing training materials, handling administration, and even troubleshooting partner issues. That fast-paced environment made me even more curious about technology, and now I’m diving deeper into product development by learning full-stack web development while teaching Mandarin.
